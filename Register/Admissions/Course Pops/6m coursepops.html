Course Overview

Ready to turn your creativity into a career? This 6-month intensive graphics design training equips you with the skills to create stunning visuals, brand assets, and print-ready designs using the industry’s most powerful tools: CorelDRAW, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
Whether you're a beginner or looking to upgrade your design skills, this course blends foundational design principles with hands-on software mastery. You’ll learn how to design logos, flyers, social media content, business cards, magazines, and more — all while building a professional portfolio that opens doors to freelance gigs, agency roles, or your own creative business. Each module is packed with real-world projects, instructor feedback, and client-style briefs to prepare you for the demands of the design industry.

Assessments, Deliverables, and Certificate

Hands-on tasks like logo design, flyer creation, photo editing, and layout exercises & short assessments at the end of each month to test software proficiency and design principles.

Group critique and feedback sessions to build confidence and improve design thinking.

A real-world client-style brief (e.g., brand identity pack or magazine layout) submitted in Month 6 for grading.
