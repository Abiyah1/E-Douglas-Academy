Course Overview

This 3-month intensive is designed for beginners and career upskillers who want usable computer skills fast. You will learn Microsoft Word for professional documents, Excel for data entry and basic analysis, PowerPoint for compelling presentations, safe and effective Internet use for research and communication, and foundational computer general studies.
Each week combines short, focused lessons with practical exercises and a weekly mini-project. By the end of the program you will produce a professional CV, a portfolio of documents and presentations, and complete a final applied task that demonstrates your new skills. Flexible online/ofline lessons, downloadable practice files, and direct instructor feedback make this course ideal for job seekers, students, and small-business owners who need immediate, applicable computer competence.

Assessments, Deliverables, and Certificate

Weekly mini-assignments and one graded module quiz every two weeks.

Peer review sessions during Week 11.

Final practical assessment in Week 12 graded pass/fail.

On completion, learners receive a certificate and a portfolio pack (CV, sample Word doc, Excel sheet, PowerPoint slides).
